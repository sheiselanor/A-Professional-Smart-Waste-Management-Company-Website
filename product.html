Our Innovative Products

Explore the cutting-edge features of our waste management solutions.

Solar System

Solar panels on smart waste bins provide a renewable source of energy. This helps to power the bin's compaction system, sensors, and communication modules without relying on external electrical sources. Solar panels contribute to the ecological benefits of the system by reducing the carbon footprint


Smart Lock System

While not detailed in the quoted sources, a smart lock system in smart waste bins can prevent overflow of the waste and ensure that waste is contained until it is fulfilled.

LCD Display

An LCD display on a smart waste bin can show various types of information such as the fill level, waste separation instructions, or educational messages. It can enhance user interaction with the bin and promote proper waste disposal practices.

Blynk Interface

Blynk is a platform that provides IoT services. A Blynk interface for smart waste bins could allow users and waste management services to monitor and control the bins remotely, providing real-time data and notifications.

Real-time Data Monitoring

Smart bins can monitor data in real-time on waste levels, types of waste, and bin status (full or empty). This data can be used to optimize waste collection time, improving efficiency and reducing operational costs
